About the Creator

Liam Niemeyer graduated from the Ohio University Honors Tutorial College in 2018 with a B.S. in Journalism, where he plans to pursue a career in the journalism industry, particularly public radio and podcasting. Timestop is a part of a professional project he created for graduation. He aspires to tell more compelling audio stories, like the ones in Timestop, in the future.
